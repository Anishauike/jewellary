Our Story

About Creative Jewellery

At Creative Jewellery, we're passionate about crafting exquisite pieces that capture the essence of individuality and personal style. With a legacy of innovation and excellence, our jewellery company has been adorning customers with unique and breathtaking designs for 70 years.

Founded by Rishika Sharma, a visionary jeweller with a keen eye for detail and a love for artistry, Creative Jewellery began as a small workshop with a big dream. Over the years, our team of skilled artisans, designers, and gemmologists has grown — but our commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction remains unwavering.

Meet Our Founder

Rishika Sharma

Meet Rishika Sharma, the visionary founder and creative force behind Creative Jewellery — a luxury jewelry brand renowned for its exquisite designs, impeccable craftsmanship, and passion for innovation.

With a natural flair for artistry and a keen eye for detail, Rishika's journey in the world of jewelry began with a childhood fascination for the intricate beauty of precious stones and metals. This early passion evolved into a career-spanning dedication to mastering the art of jewelry design.

At Creative Jewellery, Rishika's commitment to excellence, sustainability, and customer delight has fostered a culture of innovation, where tradition meets modernity, and every piece tells a story.
